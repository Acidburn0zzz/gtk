All-in-one Places

A desktop menu based on the Gnome Classic Places Menu with a few extra goodies and configuration options to change the look & feel of the applet with versions for Linux Mint Cinnamon and Gnome Shell.

  • Show or hide the sections that better fits your needs.
  • Choose between normal or collapsable menus to save vertical screen space.
  • Trash menu item with "empty trash" option.
  • Devices menu items with "eject/unmount" option.
